How to Play Circular Arrangement

Step 1

Each question presents a set of circular arrangement relationships. For instance:

  • Sand is to the right of Kangaroo
  • Cello is to the immediate right of Sand

Step 2

To solve the puzzle, arrange the subjects in a circle based on the provided premises.

  • Since "Sand is to the right of Kangaroo," place "Sand" immediately to the right of "Kangaroo" in a clockwise direction.
  • "Cello is to the immediate right of Sand," so place "Cello" immediately to the right of "Sand," continuing clockwise.

Step 3

Based on the circular arrangement you've created, you may need to evaluate the validity of a specific statement. For example, if the statement is "Kangaroo is to the immediate left of Cello," you would check your arrangement.

If your arrangement shows that "Kangaroo" is directly to the left of "Cello" (immediately counterclockwise from "Cello"), then you would click True. If not, click False.
