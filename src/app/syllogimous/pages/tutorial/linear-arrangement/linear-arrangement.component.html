How to Play Linear Arrangement

Step 1

Each question has a series of linear arrangement relationships. For example:

  • Chicken is to the immediate right of Cinema
  • Bike is to the immediate right of Chicken

Step 2

To solve the puzzle, arrange the subjects linearly based on the premises.

  • Since "Chicken is to the immediate right of Cinema," place "Chicken" immediately to the right of "Cinema."
  • "Bike is to the immediate right of Chicken," so "Bike" should be positioned immediately to the right of "Chicken."

Step 3

Based on the linear arrangement you've mapped out, you might be asked to determine the validity of a specific statement. If the question provides a new statement like "Cinema is to the immediate left of Bike," you would compare it against your linear arrangement.

If your arrangement shows that "Cinema" is to the left of "Chicken" (not directly to the left of "Bike"), you would click False. If the statement accurately reflects your arrangement, click True.
