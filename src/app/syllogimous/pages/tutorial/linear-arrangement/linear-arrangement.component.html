How to Play Linear Arrangement

Each question has a series of linear arrangement relationships. For example:

  • Chicken is to the immediate right of Cinema
  • Bike is to the immediate right of Chicken

To solve the puzzle, arrange the subjects linearly based on the premises.

If the conclusion is like "Cinema is to the immediate left of Bike," and your arrangement shows that "Cinema" is to the left of "Chicken", you would click False. If the statement accurately reflects your arrangement, click True.
