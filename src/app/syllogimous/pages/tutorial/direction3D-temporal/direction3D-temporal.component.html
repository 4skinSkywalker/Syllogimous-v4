How to Play Direction3D Temporal

Each Direction3D Temporal question presents a series of statements that describe the relative positions of objects. For example:

  • Hammer is in the future and North of Church
  • Dates is in the present and North-West of Hammer
  • Church is in the past and South-East of Dates

After understanding each object's position and their relations to each other, you need to test a conclusion. For example:

Conclusion: "Is the Church to the south-east of the Dates?"

Based on your analysis, click the True button if you believe the given relationship is correct or the False button if you think it is not.

The Negation Cue

Whenever you encounter a phrase highlighted in red, it's an indication that you need to think inversely about the information presented.
