How to Play Graph Matching

First, visualize the graph from the premises:

  • Eave originates from Leaves
  • Clothes originate from Eave

Next, visualize the graph for the conclusion:

  • Panther leads to Wander
  • Wander leads to Bell

Examine whether the two graphs are isomorphic. This means checking for a one-to-one correspondence between their vertices and edges that maintains connectivity.

If the premise and conclusion graphs match structurally, click the True button. Otherwise, click the False button.
