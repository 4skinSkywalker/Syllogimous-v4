How to Play Distinction

Distinction questions begin with a set of premises that define the relationships between various items. For example:

  • Mango is the same as Glass
  • Herbs is the same as Mango
  • Glass is the same as Shower
  • Herbs is opposite of Shower

Based on your analysis, the game might ask you to evaluate a statement like:

Conclusion: "Mango is opposite of Shower."

Based on your analysis, click the True button if you believe the given relationship is correct or the False button if you think it is not.

The Inversion Cue

Whenever you encounter a phrase highlighted in red, it's an indication that you need to think inversely about the information presented.
