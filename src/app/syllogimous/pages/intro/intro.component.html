Welcome to Syllogimous!

Syllogimous takes classic logic challenges and turns them into brain workout.

Research shows that relational training can significantly improve cognitive functions, such as language skills, problem-solving, and learning abilities. This type of training is based on Relational Frame Theory (RFT) which is a psychological theory of human language, cognition, and behaviour developed by Steven C. Hayes at the University of Nevada, Reno.

By playing Syllogimous, you're boosting your problem-solving skills, hopefully while having fun.
